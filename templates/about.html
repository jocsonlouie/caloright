About CaloRight

CaloRight is a web application used to track a user’s calorie intake every day. To meet the application objectives, useful information was added. It includes the daily consumption and the breakdown of the total nutrient. It tackles what food was taken, how many carbohydrates were consumed, proteins, fats, and the most important information, the calories consumed. To track the calories, the amount will be calculated using the basic operations.


In addition, a TDEE calculator will be included to set a user’s goal. It is a calculator to determine the number of calories that a person should consume to reach their goals like dieting, gaining weight, or being healthy. It also contains the know-how and other information about the calorie tracker, health tips, and such. With the provided features, users will be fit and healthy in the right way.
